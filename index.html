FAQs

We understand you have some questions so here is all the answers to the most frequently asked questions
What is your design and development process like?

Our design and development process typically involves gathering requirements, conducting research, creating wireframes and prototypes, iterating on the designs based on feedback, and finally implementing and testing the developed solution. We prioritize collaboration and open communication throughout the process.

Why not hire a full-time designer and developer?

Hiring full-time professionals can be daunting. It's more expensive and difficult to manage. A senior designer might cost $100k+, while a developer could surpass $200k. Combined with benefits and vacation days, it totals over $500k. Alternatively, a web design agency offers cost-efficiency, diverse skills, and flexibility without the high overhead and management issues.

Is there a limit to how many requests I can have?

Once subscribed, you can add unlimited design requests to your queue, and they will be delivered one by one.

What if I don't like the design?

No problem! We'll keep refining the design until you are completely satisfied.

Can I get a refund?

We do not offer refunds due to the dedicated time and resources allocated to each client, we are committed to flexibility. You can pause your service whenever needed and resume it later, allowing you to maximize the benefits of our collaboration.
